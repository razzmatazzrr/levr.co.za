OUR  STORY

Years ago two people became friends in an architectural office in Cape Town, their friendship sustained through their respective strange paths in life. Mr Linders (the L in LEvR) went on to Switzerland to work for a reputable architect in Zurich, gaining wonderful experience in creative design and architecture. Mrs. Ellmann van Rhyn carried on working for the architectural firm, but later moved on to find experiences in an art gallery and lecturing at UCT. Some years later, in 2014 to be more specific, after many deliberations and discussions via digital channels, the two friends came together again in Cape Town to form LEvR CREATIONS, an experimental collaboration, spanning the design sphere and focusing on playful, sustainable and creative solutions for the built environment.

WHAT WE DO

Architecture

 A design company that specializes in strategic sustainable design solutions focusing on innovative design, facilities management consultation and spatial layouts.

Furniture

We do interior architecture as well as custom and prototype furniture. At the heart of our company we aim to provide an innovative design service to all our clients that upholds both sound moral and ethical views.

Design

Design that enhances sustainablitly of the natural environment, including both the physical and psychological, without compromising an aesthetic end product
